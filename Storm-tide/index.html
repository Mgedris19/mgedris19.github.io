Why do it?

The reason that I did it is because it was a way for me to bring my imagintaion to life, so to speak. I'm not the best at art and drawing, but what I found I could do is take the drawings that others had done and then take it into one of the engines and then build my own. So if you feel like you want to let your artistic side loose but struggle with drawing it worked for me and might for you. Bellow are some of my creations: a crossbow, a Y-wing from the Star Wars franchise, and then a particle system build I made for a hack week.
