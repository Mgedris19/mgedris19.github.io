Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to show off the capabilities of the 3D modeling applications Maya and maybe Blender. I plan on having some time lapses or a video showing me working on projects alongside building the website. I also would like to show the basics of working with particle systems inside of the application. This is really just to show the fundamentals and what you can do with them inside of 3D modeling.

Audience

Peoople who want to know more about the subject and what can be done with it. The topic is very diverse and has a lot of possibilities and can kind of be a rough place to start, hopfully this will be a way for people to find a little bit of direction as they begin. This is primaraly an educational website alowing me to show people what is possible.
